1. Introduction

Arivo ("we", "us", "our") is India's Financial Decision Engine — a platform that helps you understand and act on your money. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit arivoai.in, join our beta waitlist, book a demo, or use the Arivo product during beta.

By using our website or services, you agree to this policy. Please also read our Terms & Conditions.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you interact with Arivo, we may collect:

  • Contact details — name, email address, and optionally company name when you join the beta list or request a demo.
  • Account information — login credentials and profile details if you create an Arivo beta account.
  • Usage data — how you interact with our website and app, including pages visited, features used, and device/browser information.
  • Communications — messages you send us, feedback you provide, and support requests.

3. Financial data & Account Aggregator

If you connect financial accounts during beta, we access financial information through India's RBI-regulated Account Aggregator (AA) framework. This may include bank balances, transaction history, credit card summaries, loan/EMI details, and investment holdings — only with your explicit consent.

We use this data solely to generate financial insights, due-date alerts, bill/EMI reminders, and actionable recommendations within Arivo. We do not sell your financial data.

4. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Arivo platform
  • Send beta access links, product updates, and service communications
  • Generate personalised money decisions and reminders
  • Respond to your enquiries and support requests
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for third-party advertising or automated profiling unrelated to our services.

5. Data storage & security

Your data is stored on secure infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest. We implement access controls, monitoring, and industry-standard safeguards appropriate for financial technology products.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

6. Sharing of data

We do not sell your personal or financial data. We may share information only with:

  • Service providers — trusted partners who help us operate (hosting, email delivery, analytics) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Regulated infrastructure — Account Aggregators, banks, and payment networks as required to deliver the service you consented to
  • Legal requirements — when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and users' safety

7. Cookies & analytics

Our website uses essential cookies for basic functionality. We may use privacy-conscious analytics to understand how visitors use our site. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies without your consent.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website features.

8. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may withdraw consent for financial data access at any time through the app or by contacting us. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in any email we send.

If you are in the EU/EEA or other jurisdictions with data protection laws, you may have additional rights including data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local authority.

9. Data retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Waitlist emails are removed upon unsubscribe. Financial data is deleted or anonymised when you close your account or withdraw consent, subject to regulatory retention requirements.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our product evolves. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice on our website. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at akhileshgoswami@arivoai.in.
