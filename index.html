What is Arivo OS?

Arivo OS is India's financial operating system — a unified platform for managing money across banks, mutual funds, stocks, UPI, and credit cards. Instead of switching between multiple apps and spreadsheets, Arivo brings your complete financial life into one intelligent dashboard designed for how India saves, spends, and invests.

At its core, Arivo provides real-time net worth tracking. It securely connects your financial accounts and automatically updates your assets, liabilities, portfolio performance, and available cash—giving you a complete and accurate view of your finances in one place.

Arivo also acts as an AI finance assistant built for India. It analyzes spending patterns, EMIs, bills, and investments in a local context, helping you understand cash flow, avoid missed payments, and make better financial decisions. From UPI-heavy daily expenses to long-term investment goals, Arivo helps you manage and grow your money with clarity.
